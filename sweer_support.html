FAQ

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Open the app and tap "Sign Up", then follow the prompts to complete registration.
Q: How do I post content?
A: Tap the "+" button in the center of the bottom navigation bar, select photos and edit your content, then publish.
Q: How do I edit my profile?
A: Go to the "Profile" tab and tap "Edit Profile" to update your avatar, username, and other information.
Q: How do I delete my posts?
A: Open your post, tap the menu icon in the top right corner, and select "Delete".
Q: How can I report an issue?
A: You can contact us via the email below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
